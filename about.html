About KishansKraft

Our Heritage Story

KishansKraft was born from a deep respect for traditional methods and a commitment to preserving the authentic taste of Bihar's finest mustard oil. Located in the heart of Sapta, Madhubani, we continue the age-old tradition of coldpressing mustard seeds using wooden mills (ghani) that have been passed down through generations.

Our partnership with local farmers ensures that every bottle contains the purest ingredients while supporting the rural economy. We believe in small-batch production that maintains quality over quantity, bringing you mustard oil that's not just a cooking ingredient, but a connection to Bihar's rich agricultural heritage.

How We Make Our Oil

From seed to bottle, every step follows traditional methods that preserve the natural goodness of mustard seeds

1

Seed Selection

We carefully select the finest mustard seeds from trusted local farmers in Bihar's fertile plains.

2

Cleaning & Preparation

Seeds are thoroughly cleaned and prepared using traditional methods to ensure purity and quality.

3

Cold Pressing

Using wooden ghani (traditional mills), we cold-press the seeds to preserve all nutrients and natural flavor.

4

Quality Testing

Every batch undergoes rigorous quality testing to ensure it meets our high standards for purity and taste.

5

Careful Packaging

Oil is packaged in dark bottles to protect from light, preserving freshness until it reaches your kitchen.
